Our Commitment to Accessibility

At Dale Loves Whales, we believe that music and cosmic experiences should be accessible to everyone. We've designed our website with accessibility in mind, following WCAG guidelines and constantly improving our accessibility features based on user feedback.

Additional Accessibility Features

  • All images have descriptive alt text
  • Navigation is keyboard accessible
  • Audio content has text alternatives
  • Color combinations meet contrast requirements
  • Page structure uses semantic HTML

If you encounter any accessibility issues or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us.
